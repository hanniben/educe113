Tips

How to complete the card:

  • Print it out at home or your local library.
  • Save and edit it on your phone or tablet.
  • Write it out on a blank piece of paper.
  • No need to spend more than 10-20 minutes.
  • Write and draw what YOU think is important to the topic.

Where to find inspiration:

  • Observe other cultures in your local area.
  • Pick a random place on the globe.
  • Watch point of view(POV) videos of the location you are researching.

How to research:

  • Go to your local library.
  • Read content from magazines, blogs, and news sources from around the world.
  • Search for videos on YouTube that discuss cultural norms.
