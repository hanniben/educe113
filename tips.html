Tips


Ways to fill out the card:

  • Print it out at home or your local library.
  • Save and edit it on your phone or tablet.
  • Write it out on a blank piece of paper.
  • Reconvene to discuss findings.

Research:

  • Go to your local library.
  • Read magazines and news sources from around the world.

Inspiration:

  • Observe other cultures in your local area.
  • Pick a random place on the globe.
