Culture Cards...

uses prompts to promote cultural exploration, discussion, and understanding. Each prompt begins with a personal element that links you to the topic. This link leads into to a simple research activity paired with questions to consider. The prompt ends with a creative exercise where you identify important findings.

You will be able to:

  • explore differing perspectives - by asking questions and finding answers
  • discuss cultural experiences - by identifying key aspects
  • build empathy - by relating your experiences to others
