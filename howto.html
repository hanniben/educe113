How do I use Culture Cards?

  1. Choose a prompt.
  2. Print out a card.
  3. Follow the steps, or do whatever works for you.
  4. Fill out your card.
  5. Submit your card for others to see.

What can I do with Culture Cards?

  • Take me to the library.
  • Do a group activity.
  • See what others have learned and shared.
