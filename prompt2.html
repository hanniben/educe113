Prompt 2: Dates and Holidays

Objective: Learn about a holiday and it's traditions.

Start Here:

Your birthday.

The Task:

  • Find a country with a holiday on that day or within the same month.
  • Find the purpose of the holiday.
  • Observe how people celebrate this holiday. Find any traditions.

Consider:

  • Do you have a similar event in your culture?
  • Are the traditions similar to any that you practice?
  • What are some key aspects of the event? symbolism, beliefs, etc...

Example:

La Mercè is an annual celebration held at the end of September in Barcelona, Spain.

It celebrates the Virgin of Grace, who was asked to help eradicate a swarm of locusts in Barcelona during the 1600s. When the insects eventually left, celebrations began.

Festivities include street parades, paper mache puppets, music, dance, and art.
