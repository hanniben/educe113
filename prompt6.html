Prompt 6: Senses and Attractions

Objective:

Observe what attracts people's attention around the world.

Start Here:

Go outside or sit by a window. What is the first thing you see, smell, or hear? What grabs your attention?

The Task:

  • If you live in an urban, suburban, or rural area, find a similar area in another country.
  • Create a list of possible sights, sounds, or scents that can be found in this other area.

Consider:

  • How do this list relate to what is found where you live?
  • Do these senses affect daily life in either location?
